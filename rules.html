Rules & Instructions

  1. Teams start at the entry page and proceed through questions 1 to 12.
  2. Each question page contains a riddle. Solve it to reach a campus location that has a printed QR code.
  3. Open the scanner on the question page and scan that QR code to unlock the next question.
  4. Do not tamper with QR codes. Respect campus property and safety rules.
  5. Use your phone camera or the built-in scanner. If the scanner fails, upload the QR image from your gallery.

Note: For testing you can scan the sample QR on q1 in dev mode.
